Our Products

Sweets


We make sweets with most modern technology(thermic Fluid,Steam Pressure cooking) to ensure the quality and hygiene. Indian Sweets like Kowa,Dryfruits, Bengali Sweets, Halwa, Rasamalai, Burfi, Laddus,etc items and Gulabjam will made without using any artificial colors. Sweets will be made in bulk upon orders for weddings,get togethers, kitty parties, Birthday parties and any other special occasions..
